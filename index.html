Aurora Borealis, Northern Lights

Here are some facts about the northern lights:

    The Northern Lights occur when gas particles in the Earth’s atmosphere collide with charged particles from the sun’s atmosphere.

    The different colours of the Aurora Borealis occur due to the different types of gas particles.

    Northern lights occur on the north pole of the earth, and southern lights occur on the south pole.
